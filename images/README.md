# Add Your Photos & Video Here

## Hero section (start of website)

The hero now plays a **video** instead of a photo. Add your video file:

- **`profile.mp4`** — Main video (required for video to show)
- **`profile.webm`** — Optional, for better browser support
- **`profile.png`** — Shown while the video loads (fallback if no video)

**Video tips:**
- Keep it short (5–15 seconds); it will loop
- Square or portrait works best (e.g. 400×400 or 400×600)
- Video must be muted for autoplay (browsers require this)

## Other photos

- **`profile.png`** — Fallback image and About page
- **`about.jpg`** — Photo for the About page (optional)
- **`transformation.jpg`** — Transformation section (optional)

**Supported formats:** `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`, `.mp4`, `.webm`

---

**Quick way to add files:**
- Drag and drop into this folder in Cursor's file explorer
- Or: Right-click this folder → "Reveal in Finder" → copy your files in
